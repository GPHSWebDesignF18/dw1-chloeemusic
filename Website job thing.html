Graphic Designers

A graphic designer's main job is to create something appealing to the audience's eye, or that will inspire the audience with creativity, and thought. They use computers or they create by hand, they put in a lot of work to make a design the way they like it. There are many famous artists that do graphic designs, as shown below. Graphic Designers make about $48,700 per year, and approximantly $23.41 per hour. In the year of 2016, 266,300 jobs were available for graphic designers. Outlook for the future is looking pretty good, they predict it to be growing 4%. Companies will hire graphic designers to create a logo, or create a website for them. There are many schools for graphic designers such as, Rhode Island School of Design (RISD), Parsons School of Design, The Pratt Institute, ArtCenter College of Design, Carnegie Mellon School of Design, and many others.
